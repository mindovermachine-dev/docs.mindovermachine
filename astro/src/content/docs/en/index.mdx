## A non-profit commercial foundation

**Mind over Machine** is a non-profit commercial foundation. A combined think-tank and laboratory.

Based on long-term systems thinking, we explore and test principles and techniques that can make software a regenerative tool.

In the Laboratory, we develop concrete Open Source software systems for our members and clients, based on these principles and techniques. We gather feedback and continuously adjust our assumptions, principles and techniques, which we share openly.

### Our mission

To explore and spread a new model for how software can contribute to a regenerative paradigm, which collectively contributes _positively_ to people and the World.
